# Managing Your Account

This guide covers account management, submitting contact requests, and requesting property appraisals through the Inmobiliaria Web platform.

## Contact and Appraisal System

The platform provides a comprehensive form for users to contact the real estate agency for general inquiries or property valuations.

### Accessing the Contact Form

Navigate to the contact page by:
- Clicking "Quiero Vender" in the main navigation
- Or visiting the "Contacto y Tasación" page directly

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Contact Reason">
    Choose between:
    - **Contacto General** - General inquiries
    - **Venta / Tasación** - Property sale or appraisal
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Personal Information">
    Provide your contact details so the agency can reach you.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Property Details (if applicable)">
    If requesting appraisal, provide information about your property.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Your Request">
    Review and submit your contact form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Contact Form Fields

### Personal Information (Required for All Requests)

<ParamField path="name" type="string" required>
  **Full Name** - Your complete name
  
  Used for personalized communication and record-keeping.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="email" type="string" required>
  **Email Address** - Valid email address
  
  Primary contact method. Must be a valid email format (example@domain.com).
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="phone" type="string" required>
  **Phone Number** - Your contact phone
  
  For direct communication and scheduling appointments.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="availableHours" type="string" required>
  **Available Hours** - When you can be contacted
  
  Example: "Lun-Vie 9-18hs" or "Tardes después de las 15hs"
  
  Helps schedule calls at convenient times.
</ParamField>

<Note>
  If you're logged in, your email will be automatically pre-filled from your account information.
</Note>

## General Contact Request

For general inquiries, questions about services, or other non-property-specific requests:

### Required Information

1. **Contact Reason**: Select "Contacto General"
2. **Personal Information**: Name, email, phone, available hours
3. **Message**: Describe your inquiry

### Message Field

<ParamField path="description" type="string" required>
  **Your Message** - Detailed description of your inquiry
  
  Be specific about:
  - What information you need
  - Any specific questions
  - Timeline or urgency
  - Preferred communication method
</ParamField>

<Tip>
  The more detailed your message, the better the agency can prepare for your inquiry and respond with relevant information.
</Tip>

## Property Appraisal Request

Request a professional valuation for your property if you're considering selling or renting.

### When to Request an Appraisal

- You're thinking about selling your property
- You want to know the current market value
- You're considering renting out your property
- You need a professional valuation for financial planning

### Appraisal Form Fields

<ParamField path="contactReason" type="select" required>
  **Contact Reason** - Select "Venta / Tasación"
  
  This unlocks additional property-specific fields.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="operationType" type="select" required>
  **Operation Type** - What you want to do with the property
  
  Options:
  - **Venta** - Selling the property
  - **Alquiler** - Renting the property
  - **Valuación** - Just need a valuation
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="propertyType" type="select" required>
  **Property Type** - Type of property you own
  
  Choose from: Casa, Departamento, PH, Terreno, Local, Galpón, Oficina, Campo, Depósito, Bodega, Consultorio, Fondo de comercio, Hotel, Cochera
  
  Select the option that best matches your property.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="location" type="string" required>
  **Location** - Where your property is located
  
  Be as specific as possible:
  - Street address
  - Neighborhood
  - City and province
  - Nearby landmarks
  
  Example: "Av. San Martín 1234, Centro, Rosario, Santa Fe"
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="description" type="string" required>
  **Property Description** - Detailed information about your property
  
  Include:
  - Size (covered area, lot size)
  - Number of bedrooms and bathrooms
  - Age and condition
  - Special features or recent improvements
  - Any issues or needed repairs
  - Reason for selling/renting
  
  Example: "Casa de 3 dormitorios, 2 baños, 120m² cubiertos, construida en 2010. Cocina recientemente renovada, jardín amplio, garage para 2 autos. Excelente estado general."
</ParamField>

<Warning>
  All fields marked with * are required. The form will not submit until all required information is provided.
</Warning>

## Submitting Your Request

### Form Validation

Before submission, the system validates:

- All required fields are completed
- Email format is valid
- Property fields are filled (for appraisal requests)
- Description is not empty

If validation fails:
- Red borders appear around incomplete fields
- Error messages show below each field
- Fix errors and resubmit

### Submission Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Your Information">
    Double-check all entered information for accuracy.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Submit">
    Click "Enviar Solicitud" (for appraisals) or "Enviar Mensaje" (for general contact).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    A loading spinner appears while your request is being processed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Success!">
    You'll see a success screen confirming your request was received.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### After Submission

Upon successful submission:

- Green confirmation screen appears
- Message: "¡Solicitud enviada!"
- Confirmation: "Gracias por contactarnos. Nos pondremos en contacto a la brevedad."
- Option to return to homepage

<Note>
  The agency will review your request and contact you during your available hours. Response time is typically 24-48 hours.
</Note>

## What Happens Next

### For General Contact

1. Agency receives your message
2. Staff reviews your inquiry
3. You receive a response via email or phone
4. Further communication continues based on your needs

### For Appraisal Requests

1. Agency receives your property details
2. An agent reviews the information
3. Agent contacts you to schedule an in-person visit
4. Professional appraisal is conducted
5. You receive a detailed valuation report
6. Agent discusses next steps (listing, pricing strategy, etc.)

## Form Features

### Auto-fill for Logged-in Users

If you have an account and are logged in:
- Email field is automatically populated
- Saves time on repeat contacts
- Ensures consistent contact information

### Dynamic Form Sections

The form adapts based on your selection:

- **Contacto General**: Shows only basic fields
- **Venta / Tasación**: Reveals additional property-specific fields

This keeps the form simple and relevant to your needs.

### Field Requirements

Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*):

**Always Required:**
- Name
- Email
- Phone
- Available Hours
- Description/Message

**Required for Appraisals:**
- Operation Type
- Property Type
- Location

### Cancel Option

Not ready to submit? Click "Cancelar" to:
- Return to the homepage
- Discard entered information
- Start fresh later

<Warning>
  Clicking "Cancelar" will discard all entered information without saving. Make sure to complete the form if you want to submit a request.
</Warning>

## Tips for Better Responses

    - Better initial assessment
    - More accurate preliminary valuation
    - Faster response time
    - More productive first conversation

  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Provide Realistic Available Hours">
    Consider:
    - Your actual availability
    - Time zones if relevant
    - Preferred contact method (call vs. email)

  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use a Valid Email Address">
    Ensure your email:
    - Is actively monitored
    - Has space for attachments (appraisal reports)
    - Allows emails from the domain
    - Is checked regularly
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Include Recent Improvements">
    Mention:
    - Recent renovations
    - New appliances or systems
    - Structural improvements
    - Energy efficiency upgrades
    
    These can significantly affect your property's valuation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Form Won't Submit">
  Check:
  - All required fields have values
  - Email is in valid format (user@domain.com)
  - No error messages are showing
  - Internet connection is stable
  - Try refreshing the page and resubmitting
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Not Receiving Response">
  Verify:
  - Email address is correct
  - Check spam/junk folder
  - Phone number includes country/area code
  - Available hours allow enough time for contact
  - Wait at least 48 hours before following up
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Need to Update Submitted Information">
  Solutions:
  - Submit a new request with corrected information
  - Reference your previous submission in the description
  - Call the agency directly to update details
  - Wait for agency contact and provide updates then
</Accordion>

## Privacy and Data Usage

Your information is:
- Stored securely in the agency's database
- Used only for responding to your request
- Not shared with third parties
- Protected according to privacy regulations

<Note>
  By submitting the form, you consent to the agency contacting you regarding your request. You can request removal of your data at any time.
</Note>
